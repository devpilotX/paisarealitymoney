Good to know

Questions people ask us

What is Paisa Reality?
We are a free website that helps Indian families find the government schemes and scholarships they can get, along with daily prices, bank rates and a few simple calculators. We share information. We are not financial advisors.
Is it actually free?
Yes, all of it. Every scheme, scholarship, calculator and price is free, and you do not need an account.
How do you know what I qualify for?
You tell us a few basics like your work, your state and what you need help with. We check that against each scheme's published rules and show the ones that fit.
How fresh is the information?
Prices update every day. Scheme and scholarship details, including deadlines, get reviewed on a regular basis.
Do you apply for me?
No. We show you what fits, list the documents you will need, and then send you to the official government portal to apply safely.
